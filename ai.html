Artificial Intelligence

AI allows machines to think, learn, and adapt. From facial recognition to smart assistants, it's reshaping every corner of modern life.

AI means machines or computers that can think and learn like humans. It helps computers solve problems, recognize pictures or voices, and make decisions. Examples: Chatbots like chat gpt, self-driving cars, and voice assistants like Siri or Alexa. AI learns from data — the more information it gets, the smarter it becomes.
