Computers and Progress

Computers drive every innovation — powering AI, games, research, and creativity. Their evolution defines the pace of our future.

A computer is a machine that can store, process, and display information. They help people do things like write documents, play games, design, or browse the internet. The main parts are the CPU (brain), RAM (memory), storage (hard drive), and screen. There are many types: desktops, laptops, tablets, and servers.
