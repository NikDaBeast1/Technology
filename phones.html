Phones

Phones are now mini-computers — combining AI, high-end cameras, and 5G to keep the world connected and informed.

Phones let us communicate with others by calling, texting, or video chatting. Smartphones are mini-computers that can browse the web, take photos, and run apps. Most have touchscreens, cameras, and internet access (Wi-Fi or data). Brands like Apple, Samsung, and Google make popular smartphones today.
